About T Solar

Empowering communities with clean, sustainable energy solutions.

We’re a team of young, driven Iraqis who believe in building a better future — starting with solar energy. Living through constant power cuts and rising electricity costs, we saw a simple truth: Iraq needs solar.

That’s why we started our company — to bring reliable, affordable, and clean energy to homes and businesses across the country. We work directly with top Chinese manufacturers, which allows us to offer high-quality systems at competitive prices, without the middlemen.

Our mission isn’t just to sell solar panels — it’s to help Iraq move toward energy independence. Every system we install is backed by a long-term warranty and monthly maintenance options to keep your power running smoothly for years to come.
