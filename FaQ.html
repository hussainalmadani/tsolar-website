Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about T Solar and our services.

1

What services do you offer?

We provide full solar power systems — from design and installation to testing and activation. We handle everything, including panels, inverters, batteries, wiring, and support structures.

2

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, we offer a strong warranty on all parts and services. Our systems typically include:
• 30 years on solar panels
• 15 years on inverters and batteries

3

How long does installation take?

For most homes or small businesses, installation takes 1-3 days. Larger projects may take longer, depending on size and complexity.

4

Do I need batteries with my system?

Not always. If you want backup power during outages or at night, batteries are a great option. We can install battery storage systems tailored to your needs.

5

How much will it cost?

Cost depends on the size of the system. We offer free on-site assessments to give you a custom quote based on your energy use and budget.

6

What is the system size that I need?

That depends on your average monthly electricity usage and your goals (full backup vs. partial). We’ll do a free on-site assessment to calculate the exact size and recommend the best system for your needs. In addition, our team can assess your place on-site to give a more accurate answer.

7

Can I power my whole home or business with solar?

Yes, we can design systems large enough to cover 100% of your electricity needs. We’ll calculate your average usage and build a system that fits.

8

What happens during a power outage?

With batteries, your system will automatically switch to backup mode. Without batteries, grid-tied systems shut off for safety during outages.

9

How much money will I save with solar?

Savings depend on your energy usage, local rates, and system size. Most customers see significant reductions in their electricity bills. It depends on your current bill and system size, but most clients make up for what they would spend on generators within 1-2 years.

10

How long do solar panels last?

Most panels last 30+ years, with very little drop in performance. Our warranty covers that lifespan.

11

Do I need to clean or maintain my panels?

Yes — in Iraq, dusty environment, regular cleaning is important to keep your panels working at full efficiency. That’s why we offer a monthly maintenance service that includes panel cleaning and battery health checks, so your system stays in top shape year-round.

12

What brands or equipment do you use?

We use high-quality, industry-leading brands and equipment. Contact us for details about the products we install.

13

Will solar work during cloudy days or in winter?

Yes. Solar still generates electricity on cloudy days, just at reduced output. Our systems are designed to perform year-round.

14

Can I upgrade my system later?

Absolutely. We design systems with future upgrades in mind — you can add more panels, batteries, or even EV chargers later.

15

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

We offer flexible financing and payment options to make solar more accessible. Contact us for details.

16

Can I monitor my system’s performance?

Yes. We offer smart monitoring through a mobile app or web portal, so you can track your energy generation and usage in real time.

17

How do I get started?

It’s simple — contact us for a free site assessment. We’ll check your location, electricity needs, and give you a custom plan.

18

Do you help with government approvals or paperwork?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and approvals, so you don’t have to worry about the process.

19

Is solar power worth it in Iraq?

Absolutely. Iraq has excellent sun exposure, rising electricity costs, and frequent power cuts — solar gives you independence, savings, and reliability.
